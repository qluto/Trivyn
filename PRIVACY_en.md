# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

## Overview

Triskly (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

## Information Collection and Storage

### Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Goal Data**: Daily, weekly, and monthly goal titles, completion status, and creation timestamps
- **Reflection Data**: Reflection content for each period (responses to 3 prompts)
- **Settings Data**: Application settings (week start day, language preferences, notification settings, etc.)

All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

### Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

- Providing daily, weekly, and monthly goal management features
- Displaying goal progress
- Providing reflection features
- Saving and applying application settings

## Third-Party Information Sharing

The App does not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. All data is stored exclusively on your device.

## Internet Connection

The App does not require an internet connection, except for:

- Checking for application updates (optional)

The App does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or other tracking services.

## Data Security

The App stores your data locally on your device. Data security depends on your device's security settings (password, encryption, etc.).

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the App. All local data is automatically deleted when the App is uninstalled.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. While the App can be used without age restrictions, we recommend parental supervision.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. In case of significant changes, you will be notified within the App.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Issues section of the GitHub repository.

## Consent

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
